01 The Engine

Six stages from
murky to crystal

Every image goes through an adaptive physics-guided pipeline. Strength is calibrated per-class for optimal output.

01
Classify
MobileNetV2 identifies the dominant degradation type — 9 classes, 93% accuracy.
02
Dehaze
Dark channel prior + guided filter to compute and remove the transmission map.
03
Colour
Gray World + LAB-space correction recovers the red channel lost to wavelength attenuation.
04
Fuse
Multi-scale Laplacian fusion to blend dehazing and colour-corrected streams.
05
Sharpen
CLAHE on LAB L-channel. Unsharp mask (σ=1.5). Adaptive bilateral post-denoise.
06
Upscale
Lanczos4 to 1080p. Auto-exposure normalization. 6 no-reference quality metrics computed.
